What is Bitcoin Cash (BCH)?

Bitcoin Cash is a hard-forked type of Bitcoin that first saw the light of day in August 2017. BCH shares many of Bitcoins characteristics, including reward systems, total supply, block times and protocols. But Bitcoin Cash has different block size limits and difficulty levels for blocks in comparison with the original Bitcoin.
